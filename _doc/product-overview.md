# Product Overview: Hello Veronica

## What It Is
A simple, personal landing page — a digital love letter built for one person: Veronica. Two sections, nothing more. A warm greeting and a direct declaration of love.

## Users & Audience
- **Creator**: The person who built this page (single author, anonymous to the public)
- **Recipient**: Veronica — the sole intended viewer and the reason the page exists

## Product Structure
A single-page web experience with exactly two sections:
1. **Section 1 — "Hello Veronica"**: A warm, welcoming hero section that greets Veronica by name
2. **Section 2 — "I Love You Veronica"**: A bold, clear declaration of love — the emotional centrepiece of the page

## Brand & Tone
- **Name**: Hello Veronica
- **Tone**: Warm, personal, romantic, heartfelt
- **Style**: Simple, clean, intimate — no clutter, no distractions, no competing messages
- **Palette**: Rose and warm neutrals; dark text on light backgrounds for clarity

## Scope & Positioning
- A personal tribute page, not a commercial product
- No user authentication, no backend logic, no database
- The entire purpose is to deliver one moment of connection to Veronica
- Mobile-friendly — Veronica may open this on any device, any time

## Strategic Principles
- Simplicity is the feature: two sections is exactly the right number
- The message should be the loudest, most unmistakable thing on the page
- The page belongs to Veronica — every design decision should feel like it was made for her specifically
